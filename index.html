Type any word into the search box and click "Find Rhymes." Results are grouped by syllable count so you can quickly find the perfect rhyme for your poem, song, or creative writing.

Types of Rhymes

  • Perfect rhymes — words that end with the same sound (cat/hat)
  • Near rhymes — words with similar but not identical endings (moon/June)
  • Eye rhymes — words that look alike but sound different (love/move)
  • Multi-syllable rhymes — longer rhyming matches (beautiful/dutiful)
How does the rhyme generator work?
The tool matches words based on their ending phoneme patterns — the sounds at the end of the word. It groups results by syllable count to help you find rhymes that fit your rhythm.
